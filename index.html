Stops & sightseeing
  • Horseshoe Bend, Page AZ — 15 min off US-89. Short 1.5 mi walk to iconic overlook. Don't skip it.
  • Mather Point — first major rim viewpoint after the South Entrance.
  • Bright Angel Trail — walk 1–1.5 miles down for the real canyon experience.
  • Hopi Point sunset (Hermit Rd Shuttle Stop 7) — #1 rated sunset spot. Arrive 45 min early. Sunset ~8:00–8:15 PM MST.
🌡️ The South Rim sits at 7,000 ft — mornings are a cool ~47°F even in June. Bring a layer for early walks.
